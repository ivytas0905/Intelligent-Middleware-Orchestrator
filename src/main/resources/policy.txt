COMPANY EXPENSE REINBURSEMENT POLICY
Version 2.0 -Effective since 2024

1. ACCOMMODATION
- Maximum reimbursable amount: $200 USD per night
- Only standard/business class hotels are allowed
- Luxury hotels and resorts require VP approval
- Personal guests are not covered

2. MEALS
- Maximum per day: $150 USD
- Alcohol is strictly not reimbursable
- Client entertainment meals require manager pre-approval
- Weekend meals require business justification

3. TRANSPORTATION
- FLIGHT: Economy class only. Business class requires Director approval.
  Flights over $3000 require receipt and justification.
- TRAIN: All train tickets reimbursable with receipt.
  Tickets over $1500 should justify why flight was not taken.
- TAXI/RIDESHARE: Maximum $200 per trip.
  Public transit should be considered for short distances.

4. APPROVAL THRESHOLDS
- Up to $5,000: Direct Manager approval
- $5,001 to $20,000: Director approval required
- Above $20,000: VP or C-Level approval required

5. NON-REIMBURSABLE ITEMS
- Alcohol and bar tabs
- Personal entertainment (spa, games, movies)
- Family or personal guest expenses
- First class or business class flights without pre-approval
- Weekend leisure activities
